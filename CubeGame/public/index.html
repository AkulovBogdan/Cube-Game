Rules

The player has 1 minute. Cubes appear on the playing field. They are presented in four sizes and different colors. To destroy a cube, the player needs to click on it once or twice, dependent on the type of the cube. Cubes that are destroyed by one click give 1 to 4 points for the size and 1 to 4 for the color and the cubes that are destroyed by double clicking give twice as much points. You can distinguish the type of the cube by its appearance: usual cubes are monochrome and cubes with increased experience are gradient-colored. The size of the cube affects the number of points received: the larger the cube the less points you get after destroying it. Also there is a special black cube, which adds additional seconds to the timer.
